Packaging Checklist

Information Needed Before Sampling

  • Brand name, logo file, color direction, and packaging reference.
  • Product configuration: device, cartridges, standalone tools, rollers, manual, and accessories.
  • Box size expectations, tray / inner support needs, and whether storage box or cartons are needed.
  • Destination market and language requirements for approved artwork.
