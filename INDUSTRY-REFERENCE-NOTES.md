# Industry Reference Notes

Date: 2026-06-24

Purpose: capture structural ideas from strong industry websites and translate them into this preview prototype without copying content or making unsupported claims.

## Sources Reviewed

- Cynosure Lutronic official site
  - Useful pattern: separates provider and patient audiences, groups products by type, and exposes partnership/service/support paths.
  - Applied to prototype: clearer buyer paths, product range pages, RFQ support path, and product detail pages.

- KDC/ONE official site
  - Useful pattern: presents end-to-end capability from ideation through manufacturing and packaging, plus global capability and proof-style numbers.
  - Applied to prototype: OEM/ODM capability matrix, project package checklist, packaging system structure, and buyer-ready RFQ information.

- Intercos official site
  - Useful pattern: uses "What we do", packaging, research/innovation, group footprint, and key-number style proof sections.
  - Applied to prototype: product system architecture, packaging system, factory quality matrix, and placeholder-ready proof modules.

## What Was Added

- Product system architecture matrix.
- OEM/ODM capability matrix.
- RFQ project package checklist.
- Factory control matrix.
- Packaging system matrix.
- RFQ readiness matrix.
- FAQ category guidance.
- Safer wording around quality and documentation.

## What Still Needs Real Supplier Data

- Approved social links and final public contact display rules.
- Final product model specifications and SKU compatibility.
- MOQ, sample lead time, production lead time.
- Real factory photos and product photos.
- Any verified certificates or approved certification wording.
- Real inspection records and document types.

## Compliance Note

The prototype intentionally avoids unsupported beauty-device claims, performance promises, and certification claims. Any certificate or performance-related wording should be added only after verified documents and approved wording are provided.
