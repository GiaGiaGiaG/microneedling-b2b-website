MOQ & Lead Time

Reference Planning Information

These references help buyers understand the project level before quoting. Final MOQ and timing are confirmed after model, packaging, customization, and quantity are reviewed.

Existing Model Sample1-10 pcs depending on stock; existing product samples usually prepare in about 3-7 working days.
Logo / Simple Packaging SampleMOQ applies by product and logo method; sample preparation is commonly about 7-15 working days.
Customized Packaging / Starter KitMOQ depends on box, manual, insert, tray, and product combination; sample preparation is commonly about 10-20 working days.
Batch OrderExisting model batch orders are commonly about 15-25 working days after confirmation; private label packaging orders are commonly about 20-35 working days.

Project Boundaries

What We Can and Cannot Promise

Clear boundaries help protect both the buyer and the manufacturer before sampling or batch production starts.

Can SupportExisting model wholesale, private label logo, private label packaging, starter kits, product bundles, model matching, cartridge matching, reorder supply planning, and project-based OEM/ODM support.
Cannot PromiseUnsupported regulated-use claims, guaranteed commercial outcomes, certification claims for all products, copying competitor protected designs, third-party trademarks without authorization, or no-MOQ deep customization.
Payment ReferencePayment terms can be confirmed according to order type, amount, and cooperation method.
Shipping ReferenceExpress, air freight, sea freight, customer forwarder, and market-specific logistics options can be discussed by order type.
