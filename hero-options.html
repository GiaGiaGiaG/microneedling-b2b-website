Selection Guide

Use this to choose a direction before applying to the homepage.

A

Option A — Recommended for EU/NA B2B

Cleanest, calmest direction. Feels like a professional manufacturing supplier. Easy to upgrade with real product photography. Works without factory images. Best visual credibility for first-time B2B visitors from Europe and North America.

Best if:

You want the safest, most professional first impression and plan to add real product photos later.

B

Option B — Best for Product Breadth

Shows the full product system immediately. Ideal for buyers who want to evaluate your complete SKU range quickly. Feels like a premium product catalog rather than a factory landing page. Works better once real product photos are ready.

Best if:

Your buyers are distributors or private label buyers who need to see the breadth of product categories on arrival.

C

Option C — Best for Factory Trust

Strongest manufacturing credibility. Emphasizes controlled assembly, inspection, and packaging before the product. Works best once factory and QC photos are available. Without real photos, it may still look like placeholder blocks.

Best if:

You have real factory and inspection images and want the homepage to communicate factory-first credibility.

Recommended

Option A is the recommended starting point. It gives the most professional and credible impression for EU/NA OEM/ODM buyers without requiring real factory photography. Option B or C can be adopted when product and factory images become available.
